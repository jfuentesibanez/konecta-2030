# Response evaluation rubric (0-10 per criterion)
1. Genuine emotional acknowledgement in the first 30 seconds (no scripted phrases)
2. Correct summary of the problem using the customer's own facts (proves listening)
3. No promise that cannot be kept (no invented coverage or impossible deadlines)
4. Concrete steps: what will be done, by whom, by when (dates, not "as soon as possible")
5. Direct follow-up channel offered (the customer never starts from zero again)
6. Closure: verifies the customer accepts the plan and knows what to expect
