# Helios Telecom in-sources its digital care: "we will cut vendor spend by 45%"
(ExpansionTech, 18-Jun-2026) - Operator Helios Telecom (14M customers) has announced that its new in-house
AI-agent platform will handle 60% of its digital interactions before 2028, with a projected 45% reduction in
external CX vendor spend. Complex voice and regulated complaints will remain outsourced "for now".
