# Kolibri draft evaluation rubric (0-10 per criterion)
1. Clear, descriptive use-case title
2. Owner and department identified
3. Problem quantified (volume, time, cost or quality)
4. Impact estimated with numbers, not adjectives
5. Data: identified, with sensitivity and access described
6. Solution type justified (deterministic / agentic / hybrid) with the why
7. Interface defined (where it lives: chat, email, internal system)
8. Security: sensitivity level + who accesses + compliance requirements
9. Human-in-the-loop: which decisions require human review and why
10. Honest readiness: what is missing to start (data, permissions, integration)
