# This quarter's news package (Jul-Sep 2026) — press summary
N1 (12-Jul): VoxAgent AI publishes audited metrics: 43% sustained autonomous resolution across its 40
enterprise clients for 6 consecutive months.

three large buyers say they now require "per-resolution pricing" as a mandatory option.
N3 (28-Jul): Adding up rounds and public data, the aggregate disclosed ARR of the 8 largest AI-native CX
disruptors reaches $1.9B.

care. It is the sector's second top-20 client to announce it this year, after AseguraPlus.
N5 (19-Aug): The five BPO majors publish half-year headcount: aggregate -4% vs December.
N6 (2-Sep): The European Commission opens a public consultation on a "right to human interaction in essential
services" — not yet a formal legislative proposal.
N7 (10-Sep): A telco panel reports that 1.1% of inbound already arrives via personal AI assistants.
N8 (18-Sep): Independent benchmark: a major lab's new frontier model reaches 91% on the hard tier of the
reference agentic benchmark.
N9 (25-Sep): Unions register their platform for the IV Convenio: it includes "limits to technological
substitution" among 40 demands, not declared a blocking condition.
N10 (29-Sep): A Tier-1 bank tender requires ISO 42001 from all AI-CX vendors. It is the fourth such tender
this year (of ~15 relevant ones — about 27%).
